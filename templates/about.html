Accountability: About This Data Set

The data is a compilation of the answers to a comprehensive survey given to the participants at various points throughout their lives. It contains basic information such as demographics, health, family income (or personal income once the participants are no longer dependents), to how often they feel depressed, SAT scores, and drug & alcohol habits.

Where did this data come from?

The data is from United States Department of Labor, Bureau of Labor and Statistics.

Who has access to it?

The study was performed by the Bureau of Labor and Statistics in collaboration with several other research institutions. A comprehensive explanation of the study and analysis are available for anyone to download and analyze.
